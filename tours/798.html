798 Art District

Lounge about inspiring 798 and discover the fascinating edgy art scene with her industrial nouveau riche atmosphere.

Come and join me for a tour to Beijing's edgy, fascinating contemporary art scene. It is a sprawling neighbourhood of Communist-era factories and warehouses converted into art galleries, boutiques, coffee shops and restaurants.

Once listed to be torn down, it has now become the exhibition center of Chinese culture and art. The unique architectural Bauhaus-style gives the area a distinguished ambiance of art nouveau in China with an European touch. No wonder that many artists use this area as their background for their art-work. Nowadays it frequently holds important international art exhibitions and art activities as well as fashion shows.
