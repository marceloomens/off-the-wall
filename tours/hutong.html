Hutong tour

Browse through ancient hutongs and discover Chinese lifestyle; their beliefs and routines.

This tour will take place in the heart of the city at the North East side of the Forbidden City. We will wander through some ancient hutongs where you will be surprised by the tranquil atmosphere. No rush, no noisy cars and no high-rises; you will totally forget the fact that we are in a Capital City of the second World Economy. We will discover the local trades in the small alleys and if you like we can try some local treats. History is still so visual
