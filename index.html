Discover the hidden hutongs and learn about the Chinese with Off the Wall Tours of Beijing.

Do you want to visit the best places in Beijing? Do you prefer to go on foot or by bike? Do you want to learn more about Chinese culture and history? Do you want a glance at the daily life of the Chinese?

You've come to the right place

My name is Mirella Coolen and I am a Beijing tour guide. I aim to visit the hidden places that are not standard. You are most welcome on one of my group tours. Check the calendar, or email me to book a private tour.

On my tours you will discover the contrast between the richness of Chinese history and the modern China. Explore how East met West by strolling through the ancient hutongs downtown or by cruising the lakes in the heart of the city. Get overwhelmed by communist architecture, experience imperial power, learn how deeply Feng Shui is embedded in the heart of the Chinese people.

I look forward to meeting you somewhere in Beijing.

Mirella
